About RWYEngine

RWYEngine is a writing companion designed to help authors track characters, locations, items, events, story relationships, and detailed worldbuilding notes in one place. It acts like a memory engine: you store your story information, and RWYEngine recalls it instantly whenever you need it.

Features

  • Organize story elements with editable entries
  • Fields for title, description, category, tags, and synonyms
  • Search entries quickly by title
  • Manage relationships between characters, places, or concepts
  • Interactive relationship map with draggable nodes and curved edges
  • Tree or force-directed graph layouts
  • Click a node to jump directly to its entry
  • Export the relationship map as a PNG
  • Automatic saving when switching entries

How to Use

  1. Create entries for characters, locations, items, events, or ideas.
  2. Add descriptions, categories, tags, or alternate names.
  3. Use the search bar to quickly find entries.
  4. Open the Relationship Editor to connect entries meaningfully.
  5. Switch to the Relationship Map to visualize your story structure.
  6. Drag nodes to reposition your story graph dynamically.
  7. Export the map as a PNG for planning or documentation.

RWYEngine stays lightweight and focused on organization to help you build your world efficiently.
